Article Submission:<\/span><\/p>\n

Articles in French, German, or English are invited for submission to IJGLSA<\/em>. They should include an abstract at the beginning of no more than 100 words and a 2-3 sentence biographical note at the end. Follow the Guidelines for Authors<\/a> and submit two copies with a disk. (Manuscripts cannot be returned).<\/p>\n

Please submit articles\/correspondence to:<\/p>\n

<\/div>\n

Irmengard Rauch
\n5414 Dwinelle Hall
\nUniversity of California, Berkeley
\nBerkeley, CA 94720-3243<\/p>\n

IJGLSA Peer Review Information:<\/span><\/p>\n

Submitted articles are peer reviewed by three or more experts in the subject matter to provide feedback on whether the article is of a publishable quality. If the peer review is positive, the editor informs the article’s author of its acceptance contingent upon revision of the article based on reviewers’ and editorial suggestions\/comments.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
