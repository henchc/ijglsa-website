Submission

Article Submission:

Articles in French, German, or English are invited for submission to IJGLSA. They should include an abstract at the beginning of no more than 100 words and a 2-3 sentence biographical note at the end. Follow the Guidelines for Authors and submit two copies with a disk. (Manuscripts cannot be returned).

Please submit articles/correspondence to:

Irmengard Rauch
5414 Dwinelle Hall
University of California, Berkeley
Berkeley, CA 94720-3243

IJGLSA Peer Review Information:

Submitted articles are peer reviewed by three or more experts in the subject matter to provide feedback on whether the article is of a publishable quality. If the peer review is positive, the editor informs the article’s author of its acceptance contingent upon revision of the article based on reviewers’ and editorial suggestions/comments.
